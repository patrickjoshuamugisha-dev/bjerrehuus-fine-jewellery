- **Pillow collection** is the first launch series — make it real from day 1.
- **Wishlist** is explicitly phase 2 — Vibe said it herself at 30:48.
- **Hellere rigtigt end hurtigt** — this is her core value. Don't ship anything that looks cheap or rushed.
- **Communication with Patrick:** email (stylesnob mail) + phone. Not Upwork chat.
- **Vibe's background:** fashion/design production since 2003. Co-founded Mumbai factory 2010. Five years of legal battles post-divorce. Just remarried. Starting fresh with this brand — it matters deeply to her.
