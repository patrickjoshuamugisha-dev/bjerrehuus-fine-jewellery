e-conomic Accounting Sync Required
Shopify App Store → "E-conomic link" by CarryTheOne
  • Go to Shopify Admin → Apps → Shopify App Store
  • Search for "e-conomic" — install the app called "E-conomic link" by CarryTheOne (this is the correct app — it handles Danish/EU VAT correctly)
  • Open the app and click Connect to e-conomic
  • Log in with your e-conomic credentials (OAuth — uses your e-conomic login, not Shopify)
  • Follow the setup wizard: map Shopify product groups → e-conomic product groups
  • Enable automatic invoice creation on order completion
  • Place a test order and confirm an invoice appears in e-conomic
What this does: Automatically creates invoices in e-conomic for every Shopify order, with correct Danish VAT (25% moms). Required for accounting and VAT reporting. OAuth login cannot be done via code — must be done manually.
3
EU VAT / OSS Scheme Required
Admin → Settings → Taxes and duties
  • Go to Shopify Admin → Settings → Taxes and duties
  • Scroll to the European Union section
  • Click Manage next to EU taxes
  • Enable the OSS (One Stop Shop) scheme — this lets you collect correct VAT for each EU country without registering in each separately
  • Enter your Danish CVR / VAT number when prompted
  • Click Save
What OSS does: Automatically applies the correct VAT rate per EU country on orders. Required if selling to EU customers outside Denmark.
