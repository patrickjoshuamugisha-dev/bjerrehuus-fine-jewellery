## Current Scope

Launch is English-only, delivery-only, with Denmark free shipping and International shipping at 299 DKK.

Blocked/client-dependent work:
- Shopify Payments/KYC, MobilePay, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay.
- Transactional email templates after account/email verification.
- Shopify Forms after account/email verification.
- Domain connection.
- Final QA with one real order/refund and one real inquire test.

Removed from launch scope: Danish translations/Translate & Adapt, e-conomic, Klaviyo, local pickup.
