Tasks for Vibe  ·  May 2026  ·  These steps require your personal login or bank details — only you can complete them

These are the only tasks that cannot be handled by Patrick. Each one requires either your personal MitID, your bank account details, or your verified Shopify owner email. They take around 20–30 minutes in total and can be done from any computer — just go to your Shopify Admin.
1
Verify Your Store Email
Check your inbox
  • Shopify has sent a verification email to the store owner email address
  • Find this email (check spam if needed) and click the verification link
  • That's it — this unlocks the Notifications section so the branded email templates can be applied
Why this is needed: Shopify locks email notification editing until the store owner verifies their email. The branded order/shipping confirmation templates are ready and waiting — Patrick will apply them as soon as you've verified.
2
Set Up Shopify Payments
Shopify Admin → Settings → Payments
  • Go to shopify.com, log in to your store, then go to Settings → Payments
  • Click Complete account setup under Shopify Payments
  • Fill in your business details: CVR number, business address, and bank account (IBAN) for payouts
  • Complete identity verification — Shopify will ask for your personal ID (passport or national ID)
  • Once approved, click Manage next to Shopify Payments
  • Under Wallets, make sure these are all ticked: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay
  • Click Save
Payout timing: Once set up, Shopify Payments pays out to your bank account on a rolling 3-day schedule. You'll get an email confirmation when your first payout is scheduled.
3
Enable MobilePay
Shopify Admin → Settings → Payments → Manage
  • After completing Shopify Payments setup (step 2 above), stay on the Payments page
  • Click Manage next to Shopify Payments
  • Scroll to Wallets and find MobilePay
  • Toggle MobilePay on — you will be asked to verify with your Danish business bank credentials (MitID)
  • Complete the MitID verification
  • Click Save
Why MobilePay matters: The majority of Danish customers prefer to pay with MobilePay. Without it, a large share of visitors will abandon checkout. This is one of the most important steps before going live.
4
Confirm Where the Domain Is Registered
Your domain account or Shopify Admin → Settings → Domains
  • Confirm whether you already own the Bjerrehuus domain
  • If yes, tell Patrick where it is registered, for example Shopify, GoDaddy, Namecheap, or another registrar
  • If it is outside Shopify, either share the registrar login with Patrick or be ready to add the DNS records Patrick sends you
  • If no domain exists yet, tell Patrick so he can buy one through Shopify
5
Approve Shipping Rates
Confirm with Patrick
  • Current setup: Denmark is free shipping
  • Current setup: International shipping is 299 DKK
  • Tell Patrick if these should change before launch
6
Final Store Review and Launch Date
Preview link from Patrick
  • Walk through the preview link Patrick sends you
  • Approve product copy, images, pricing, and the English-only launch setup
  • Confirm the launch date before Patrick removes the storefront password
